# VibeNovel Agent Rule — AI Writing Engine Guardrails

Planner may know future story direction.
Writer must not receive raw future outline, hidden truth, unrevealed twist, ending, or full reveal schedule.

All prose generation must go through:

1. Canonical Story State
2. Character Knowledge
3. Reveal Gate
4. Context Packet
5. Beat Contract
6. Validator
7. Prose Versioning
8. User Accept/Edit
9. Chapter Delta

AI output must not become canon automatically.

New facts must be:

- proposed,
- validated,
- accepted,
- then stored.

Never allow the writer to invent major canon facts such as:

- secret family relation,
- pregnancy,
- death,
- betrayal,
- identity reveal,
- world rule,
- timeline-changing event,
- important object,
- major relationship change.
