The challenge

Writing long fiction is harder than writing the first chapter.

Most AI writing tools are optimized for short outputs. Long-running stories introduce a different challenge: maintaining continuity across dozens or hundreds of chapters.

Lost Context

AI gradually forgets important story details as the project grows.

Inconsistent Characters

Characters suddenly behave differently from their established personalities.

Premature Reveals

Future twists and secrets leak before the story is ready.

Filler Chapters

Chapter progression slows down and momentum disappears.

Canon Drift

The story slowly diverges from its own established rules and history.

The solution

Built specifically for serial storytelling.

Narraza is designed around story continuity, controlled information flow, and long-form narrative management.

Canon-First Memory

Keep important story facts anchored so the narrative remains consistent over time.

Safe Context Packets

Provide AI with only the information needed for the current chapter.

Reveal Gates

Protect future twists, secrets, and hidden information until the correct moment.

Character Knowledge Guard

Ensure characters only know what they should know inside the story.

Chapter Momentum Framework

Maintain progression, tension, and narrative movement chapter after chapter.

Publishing Workflow Support

Prepare stories for serialization and publication with structured outputs.
